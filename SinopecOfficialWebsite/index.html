About Us

Sinopec International Petroleum Exploration and Development Corporation("SIPC"), a wholly-owned subsidiary of SINOPEC Group, is specialized in over-seas oil and gas investment and operations. SIPC was established in January2001, headquartered in Beijing and consists of 19 departments and 28 over-seas companies. The primary business of SIPC includes the following:Investments in the exploration, development, production, sale, storage andtransportation of oil and gas.

China Petrochemical Corporation (SINOPEC), as the parent company of SIPC,is a super-large petroleum and petrochemical group established in July 1998on the basis of the former China Petrochemical Corporation, headquartered inBeijing. Sinopec Group has a registered capital of RMB 182 billion.
